Message from the Minister of Business Development and Tourism, The Hon. Patrice K. Minors, JP, MP

The people of Bermuda look forward to sharing the unique beauty and vibrant culture of our island paradise with you. No matter if it's enjoying our pink sand beaches or scuba diving, golf or spa, great food or breathtaking sights, business meetings or destination weddings, start dreaming of your perfect escape to the other side of the world—only 2 hours away from most East Coast US airports. Because, as Mark Twain once said about Bermuda, "You may go to heaven if you want to-I'd rather stay here."
