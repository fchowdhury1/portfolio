Who says meetings have to be dull? Host your next business meeting, conference or incentive programme in the land of pink sand and crystal clear waters and watch your productivity skyrocket. Only two hours from the east coast, Bermuda is the perfect destination for your company. Welcome to the perfect blend of business and pleasure.

Why Bermuda?

Why not? Meetings in Bermuda are at once accessible, efficient, tax deductable, simple, easy and accommodating. We can't think of a single reason not to schedule your next business venture on our side of the world.

Learn More

Hotels with Meeting Facilities

Not only are our hotels welcoming, luxurious and convenient, but our meeting facilities are top notch and state-of-the-art. Take your pick of a location you won't want to leave whether for work or for play.

Learn More

Off-Site Venues

We offer a wide range of flexible function rooms in exquisite locales all over the island. Reserve one of these venues and give your attendees something to remember.

Learn More

Destination Management Companies

Nobody knows the ins and outs of meeting planning better than on-island destination management companies. From pre-planning to post-event, you can trust them to eliminate the stress and make your business their business.

Contact the Groups & Incentives Sales Team

The Bermuda Department of Tourism's Group & Incentive Sales Department is here to help with hotel selection, programme planning, promotional materials, audiovisual support, or any other questions you may have about bringing your next meeting to paradise.

Message from the Minister of Business Development and Tourism, The Hon. Patrice K. Minors, JP, MP

The people of Bermuda look forward to sharing the unique beauty and vibrant culture of our island paradise with you. No matter if it's enjoying our pink sand beaches or scuba diving, golf or spa, great food or breathtaking sights, business meetings or destination weddings, start dreaming of your perfect escape to the other side of the world—only 2 hours away from most East Coast US airports. Because, as Mark Twain once said about Bermuda, "You may go to heaven if you want to-I'd rather stay here."
