Hit the links with views that you'll remember for a lifetime. Swing away under clear blue skies. Play some of the most prestigious beachside courses on our side of the world. And do it all in five days or less.

Ultimate Golf Vacation Itinerary

Below is a suggested itinerary for your ultimate golfing vacation. Get a sampling of our courses by following the first three days, or go for the green with our full five day itinerary. Either way, you'll be on par for any golfer's dream vacation. Or click here to learn more about all of Bermuda's golf courses and annual tournaments.

Day Two - Find Your Groove

Head to Port Royal Golf Course, home of the PGA Grand Slam of Golf 2008, 2009 and 2010. Play a round like the pros and enjoy the spectacular views. Relax and enjoy a delicious meal at their ocean view bar and grill 64°.

Day Three - Master The Greens

Riddell's Bay Golf & Country Club located in Warwick has had golfers marveling at the requirements of accuracy needed to master this tight course. Built on a 600-acre peninsula, this course offers amazing water views at many holes.

Day Four - To the Back Nine

Do not let this par 54 course fool you. The heavily bunkered greens and water hazards at the Fairmont Southampton's 18 hole executive course offers challenging short play focusing on accuracy rather than power.

Day Five - The 18th Hole

To round out your five amazing days of golf, a challenging game at the Belmont Hills Golf Club is a must. The par 70 course features faster TifEagle greens and man-made lakes separated by a waterfall. What a fantastic way to end your golf holiday.

We're Accessible

Bermuda is closer than you think: less than two hours from most East Coast US gateway cities, two and a half hours from Toronto and less than seven hours from London. Travel to Bermuda in the morning or afternoon and hold your first meeting or social event the same day.
