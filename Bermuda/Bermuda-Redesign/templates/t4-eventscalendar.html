Bermuda Bridge Club Games - 2010

Bermuda Bridge Club, Pomander Road, Paget (East End)

Mind Sports: The Bermuda Bridge Club runs regular bridge games that are open to anyone including visitors. The Club plays Duplicate Contract Bridge. Games are open to all except on Wednesday and Thursday evenings, which are restricted to players with less than 300 and 100 Master points.

Admission: $10 per game for non-members. Games are played all year round and are subject to Christmas and New Year holidays.

ENQUIRIES

www.bermudabridge.com

December 1, 8, 2010
2:30 - 4:00pm

Bermuda Cookery Demonstration - 2010/11

No. 6 Passenger Terminal, Front Street, City of Hamilton (East End)

Cultural Events: Bermudian cuisine is a 'melting pot' of ethnic influences. Bermudian chef, Cheryl Kerr, shares local flavours.

Admission: Free. This is a Bermuda Rendezvous Activity.

Enquiries: Department of Community and Cultural Affairs, 81 Court Street, Hamilton, tel 441 292 9447, or visit one of our Visitor Information Centre's: next to the Ferry Terminal, Front Street, Hamilton, tel 441 295 1480; in the World Heritage Centre, Penno's Wharf, Town of St. George, tel 441 297 5791; in Gazebo #2, Dockyard Terrace, Royal Naval Dockyard, tel 441 238 4842

December 1, 3, 7-8, 10, 2010
12:00pm - 2:00pm

Bermuda Gardens Tour - 2010/11

Botanical Gardens Visitor's Centre, Paget (East End)

Cultural Events: Meet at the Berry Hill entrance near Botanical Garden's Visitor's Centre. Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Fridays, 10:30am. Sub-tropical fruit groves, cedar and banyan trees, formal gardens, sensory garden, cacti and display houses with exotic plants are a few of the 15 permanent collections in the lush 36 acres.

On Tuesdays and Fridays only from 12:00pm - 2:00pm while in the Botanical Gardens, visit historic 'Camden' the official residence of the Premier, providing official functions are not scheduled.

Admission: Free

ENQUIRIES

Department of Parks, Botanical Gardens, 169 South Rose, Paget DV 04, tel 441 236 5902, www.gov.bm
