Facility

An exclusive, romantic resort with 94 cottage-style rooms and suites for the ultimate in privacy and luxury, including three exclusive private pool suites, unique to Bermuda. A stunning outdoor infinity pool complex offers Shutters, our casual dining option; award winning Tamarisk restaurant for fine dining; Breezes for beach dining. Exclusive private island and candlelight beach dinners available on request. Indoor pool at the Aquarian Baths; Ocean Spa for traditional and holistic therapies, plus European face and body treatments. Complimentary ferry to Hamilton.

Dining

Awaken a new craving with sumptuous cuisine at Cambridge Beaches. Presenting an array of tantalizing options at glorious locations, from international and French infused cuisine at the Tamarisk Room overlooking Mangrove Bay, to the casual open-air beach-front restaurant Breezes, to the poolside Shutters café, Cambridge Beaches understands the importance of great taste.

Entertainment

Indoor and outdoor dancing (seasonal), day and evening excursions, cruises and dine around program.

Sports

While the resort offers plenty of activities for guests, there are also a variety of daily excursions that will allow you to enjoy the best of Bermuda, rent a bike or moped, horseback riding, or explore a myriad of cultural and historical sites. The ferry service in Bermuda is a remarkable excursion all of its own, our front desk will be happy to advise about schedules from nearby Royal Naval Dockyard.
