You're Invited To The Unexpected

Everything on our island is something to write home about, it's just a matter of which elements are the ones that will make your vacation shine. No matter if you're looking for your heart to race or your muscles to relax, Bermuda can offer you everything you expect and some things you don't..

Signature Events

Amongst the multitude of events Bermuda hosts annually, a few stand out as our "Signature Events". These events are usually recurring, usually the largest and most popular events on our island and always spectacularly planned.

Learn More

Activities

No matter what season, everything you could want to do on your perfect vacation is at your fingertips in Bermuda. Golf, shopping, museums, sightseeing, scooter adventures, snorkeling, scuba diving, bike rentals, horseback rides, beaches, restaurants, and all offered to you with a big Bermudian smile.

Learn More

Dining

From chic international cuisine in posh dining rooms to conch stew at a roadside café, to a literal beachfront meal at sunset, we have a taste to please every palate and budget. Stay awhile and refine that palate while you sample a little of everything Bermuda has to offer your tastebuds..

Learn More

Nightlife

Theatre and dance performances, live music, the latest films, swank martini lounges and welcoming pubs. While we do value island tranquility, we like to have a bit of fun too, and with so much to do in the evenings on this surprising island paradise, you might just need to schedule an afternoon nap.

Learn More

Golf

The hardest part for even the most avid golfer in Bermuda might just be keeping your eye on the ball. Each one of our award-winning courses is surrounded by even more breathtaking scenery that you'll wish that the game and your trip each never had to end.

Learn More

Spa

Relaxation is Bermuda's middle name. If you're looking for it, you can find it anywhere you are, but nowhere more than in one of our many island spas. Wash your cares away and prepare to go refreshed into another exhilarating night.

Learn More

Transportation

Once you arrive in Bermuda by air, cruise or private vessel, you will be able to easily travel the entire island by foot, scooter, bus or taxi. So, sit back and let someone else worry about directions for a change.

Learn More

Message from the Minister of Business Development and Tourism, The Hon. Patrice K. Minors, JP, MP

The people of Bermuda look forward to sharing the unique beauty and vibrant culture of our island paradise with you. No matter if it's enjoying our pink sand beaches or scuba diving, golf or spa, great food or breathtaking sights, business meetings or destination weddings, start dreaming of your perfect escape to the other side of the world—only 2 hours away from most East Coast US airports. Because, as Mark Twain once said about Bermuda, "You may go to heaven if you want to-I'd rather stay here."
