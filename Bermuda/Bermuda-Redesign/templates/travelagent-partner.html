Travel Agents & Partners

Working with a travel professional such as an agent on tour operator can be an excellent alternative to arranging your own travel plans. Our professionals will take care of all the travel particulars, leading to:

  • Less hassle and guesswork on your part
  • Great savings and special deals
  • Plans that cater to your specific interests

There are three types of travel professionals:

Travel Agents

Travel agents help their customers arrange travel plans and deliver personalized, professional advice, as well as time and cost savings. Many people choose to work with an agent due to the one-on-one relationship and support.

Bermuda Specialists

Bermuda specialists are travel agents with special training in travel to our island by the Bermuda Department of Tourism. These professionals have in-depth knowledge of our island and owrk to ensure their expertise remains up to date.

Tour Operators

Tour operators offer multi-item packages. These could include air or cruise travel, accommodations, restaurants and local attractions for one or more destinations. Typically, these packages are also available through travel agents, but now several tour operators will serve you directly.
