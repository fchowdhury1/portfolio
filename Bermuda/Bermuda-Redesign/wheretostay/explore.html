Cambridge Beaches Resort & Spa

Sandys Parish (West End)

The AAA Four Diamond Cambridge Beaches Resort & Spa, a luxurious timeless sanctuary of luxurious cottage accommodations, sparkling azure seas, endless views and exquisite beaches.

Elbow Beach, Bermuda

Paget Parish (Central)

Elbow Beach, Bermuda is a luxurious resort where guests enjoy personalized service and privacy is assured. The hotel is set amongst 50 acres of tropical gardens that gently slope down to a pink sand beach.

Learn More

The Fairmount Southampton

Southampton Parish (West End)

The Fairmont Southampton is situated on Bermuda's highest point and overlooks lush tropical gardens, pink sandy beaches, azure blue seas and spectacular sunsets making it 'the world's most beautiful island destination'.

Learn More

The Fairmont Hamilton Princess

Pembroke Parish (Central)

The Fairmont Hamilton Princess is located on the shores of downtown Hamilton's picturesque harbour. It is an elegant luxury resort that is a tribute to the old world splendour of the island.

Learn More

Grotto Bay Beach Resort & Tennis Club

Hamilton Parish (East End)

The Grotto Bay Beach Resort sits on a lush tropical estate along the water's edge in Bailey's Bay. Eleven brightly coloured, traditional Bermudian cottages overlook turquoise waters dotted with small islands.
